welcome to pricepulse.

you opened this tab because you just installed pricepulse. here is what it does, what it doesn't do, and what we collect.

what it does

  • you add competitor pricing pages (or any page you want monitored) to your watchlist by clicking the toolbar icon while you are on the page.
  • our backend re-fetches each watchlist URL on your tier schedule (weekly free, daily Pro, hourly Pro+).
  • when text on the page changes vs the previous snapshot, we email you a one-line summary + a link to the side-by-side diff.
  • you can compare any two snapshots in the diff viewer and export your data as CSV (Pro / Pro+).

what it does NOT do

  • we do not scrape pages from your computer — all fetching happens server-side. your browser is never the user-agent.
  • we do not auto-buy, auto-cancel, or change anything on the pages we monitor. read-only by design.
  • we do not track your browsing — we only see URLs you explicitly add to your watchlist.
  • we do not give buy/avoid recommendations. just diffs.

what we collect

  • your watchlist URLs — required, to know what to monitor.
  • your alert email — optional, only if you want email alerts.
  • an anonymous install id — a 24-character random string, used only as a per-install rate-limit key on the Free tier. never linked to your identity.
  • nothing else. no analytics, no fingerprinting, no third-party trackers in the extension.

full privacy policy: extensions.voiddo.com/pricepulse/privacy/
